Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. When you input your API key, it's only used locally in your browser and never sent to or stored on any server.

Your usage data is fetched from the OpenAI API, and it's used only to display the charts in your browser. It's not stored or shared in any way.

Remember, the key stays in your local storage until you manually clear it or use the browser's private/incognito mode.
